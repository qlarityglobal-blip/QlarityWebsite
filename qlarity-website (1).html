Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

What does my team actually need to do? +
Minimal involvement. Before go-live: answer five questions (two minutes) and forward your business number to the dedicated Twilio line we set up for you — takes five minutes, we walk you through it. After go-live: your team changes nothing. The system runs entirely on our side. You receive a weekly report and have access to a live dashboard whenever you want to check in.
How quickly will I actually see results? +
Most operators see their first recovered lead within the first week of pre-onboarding — before the full system even goes live. We manually activate the missed call layer immediately after signing. By day 14 post go-live you have attributable numbers across all three core layers. The dormant reactivation campaign fires for the first time at month two and is typically where operators see their largest single recovery event.
What software does my team need to learn? +
None. The system runs entirely on our infrastructure. Your team logs into nothing new and changes nothing about how they handle calls, estimates, or completed jobs. You receive one dashboard URL — bookmark it and check it whenever you want. That's the extent of the interface on your side.
How is the recovered revenue attributed? +
We report in three clear categories. Directly Recovered: the system triggered a response that led to a traceable booking — missed call replied, estimate responded to, dormant customer rebooked. Influenced: the system touched the prospect at some point before a booking occurred. Activity Generated: the automation fired and a conversation opened, outcome still in progress. We never claim every dollar as a direct recovery — only what's genuinely traceable. Operators consistently find the direct recovery figure alone justifies the investment many times over.
Is there a contract? What if I want to cancel? +
There is a three-month minimum term. This exists because the system needs 90 days to show its full picture — the missed call layer produces results in week one, but the dormant reactivation campaign fires at month two and the estimate follow-up needs a complete cycle to demonstrate its compounding impact. After three months it is month-to-month. Our guarantee: if the system is not fully live by your agreed go-live date, we continue supporting you at no additional charge until it is.
What size operation is this built for? +
Residential HVAC operators with five to twenty trucks and an established inbound call flow. You need sufficient call volume, estimate activity, and an existing customer base for the three core layers to produce strong numbers. We run a short qualification on the audit call to confirm it's genuinely a good fit — we won't take on operators where the ROI won't be clear and significant.
What happens on the free audit call? +
A 30-minute call with our founder. We use your actual numbers — call volume, estimate flow, customer database size, average job value — to calculate your specific revenue exposure across all three leak categories. You see the exact figure on screen. No pitch until you've seen your number. If it doesn't make financial sense for your operation at this stage, we'll tell you directly on the call.
