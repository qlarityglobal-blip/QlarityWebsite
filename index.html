What does my team actually need to do?+
Minimal involvement. Before go-live: answer five questions (two minutes) and forward your business number to the dedicated line we set up for you — five minutes, we walk you through it. After go-live: your team changes nothing about how they handle calls, estimates, or completed jobs. The system runs entirely on our side.
How quickly will I see results?+
Most operators see their first recovered lead within the first week of pre-onboarding — before the full system even goes live. We manually activate the missed call layer immediately after signing. By day 14 post go-live you have attributable numbers across all three core layers. The dormant reactivation campaign fires at month two and is typically where operators see their largest single recovery event.
What software does my team need to learn?+
None. The system runs entirely on our infrastructure. Your team logs into nothing new and changes nothing about how they operate. You receive one dashboard URL — bookmark it and check whenever you want. That's the entire interface on your side.
How is recovered revenue attributed?+
We report in three categories: Directly Recovered (system triggered a traceable booking), Influenced (system touched the prospect before a booking occurred), and Activity Generated (automation fired and a conversation opened). We never claim every dollar as a direct recovery — only what's genuinely traceable. Clients consistently find the direct figure alone justifies the investment many times over.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?+
There is a three-month minimum term. The system needs 90 days to show the full picture — missed call recovery produces results in week one, dormant reactivation fires at month two, and estimate follow-up needs a complete cycle. After three months it's month-to-month. Guarantee: if the system isn't fully live by your agreed go-live date, we continue supporting at no additional charge until it is.
What size operation is this built for?+
Residential HVAC operators with five to twenty trucks and an established inbound call flow. You need sufficient call volume, estimate activity, and an existing customer base for the three core layers to produce strong numbers. We run a short qualification on the audit call — we won't take on operators where the ROI won't be clear and significant.
What happens on the free audit call?+
A 30-minute call with our founder. We use your actual numbers — call volume, estimate flow, customer database size, average job value — to calculate your specific revenue exposure across all three leak categories. You see the exact figure on screen during the call. No pitch until you've seen your number. If it doesn't make financial sense, we'll say so directly.
